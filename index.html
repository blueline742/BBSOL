🚀 BBBuyBot Development Announcement

We're bringing a brand new buybot to the market! BBBuyBot 🤖

What's special about this bot?

  • Automated Buy Comps: Devs prepay before the comp starts, set the comp time and holding period. The bot auto-pays the winner if they're still holding. No more rugs!
  • Global Raffle: Live raffles with growing prize pools. Buy tickets via the bot in any Telegram. Countdown timer, live draws, and winners shown on the main bot page.
  • Automated House Raffle: Standard raffle, but fully automated. Devs pay upfront, bot pays winner based on hold time.
  • BB Trending: Once live, we'll develop a custom trending platform with unique features to grow our reach.

This is a massive step for #BB - Not only exposure but revenue from the bot itself as we take a % from the global raffle!
